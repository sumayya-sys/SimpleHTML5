Cyber

Common security measures include strong passwords, multi-factor authentication, encryption, firewalls, and regular software updates. Cybersecurity is crucial for individuals and organizations to prevent identity theft, financial loss, and operational disruptions. Careers in this field range from security analysts and penetration testers to digital forensics specialists, making it an essential and growing area in today's technology-driven world.

Security

Cybersecurity refers to the practice of defending computers, servers, mobile devices, networks, and data from malicious attacks. It ensures confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information. It focuses on maintaining confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information while defending against threats like malware, phishing, ransomware, and data breaches.
